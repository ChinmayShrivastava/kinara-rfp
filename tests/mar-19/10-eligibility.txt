Based on the excerpt provided, the eligibility criteria for the RFP are as follows:

1. Domestic Entities:
- Institutions of higher education
- For-profit entities
- Nonprofit entities
- State and local governmental entities, US territories, and Indian tribes
- Consortia of entities described above
- Must be organized, chartered, or incorporated under the laws of a particular state, territory, or tribe of the United States
- Must have majority domestic ownership and control
- Must have a physical place of business in the United States

2. Ineligible Entities:
- NETL (National Energy Technology Laboratory)
- DOE/NNSA FFRDCs as prime recipients
- Non-DOE/NNSA FFRDCs as prime recipients
- Federal agencies and instrumentalities (other than DOE) as prime recipients
- Entities banned from doing business with the U.S. government
- Nonprofit organizations described in Section 501(c)(4) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 that engaged in lobbying activities after December 31, 1995

3. Foreign Entities:
- May be approved for a waiver in limited circumstances
- Must submit a Full Application accompanied by a written waiver request
- Must include specific information in the waiver request (listed in Appendix C)
- The applicant does not have the right to appeal DOE's decision concerning a waiver request.
